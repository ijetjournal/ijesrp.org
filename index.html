International Journal of Scientific Engineering and Research Development

    Welcome to International Journal of Scientific Engineering and Research Development is an international, peer-reviewed open access Journal of Scientific and Engineering Research published in English. The Journal's publisher is the International Journal of Scientific Engineering and Research Development. The Journal aims at publishing evidence-based, scientifically written articles from different disciplines of sciences and Engineering Research. The Journal welcomes articles of general interest to audiences of Scientific and Engineering researchers especially when they contain new information.. The goal of IJSERD is to promote scientific information interchange between researchers, developers, students, and practitioners working in and around the world.
